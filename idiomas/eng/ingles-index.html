NGO Objectives

The objective of "PCI: Commitment and Inclusion Project" is to promote equal opportunities and the inclusion of people of color in various areas of society. This includes actions such as raising awareness about racial discrimination, promoting inclusive public policies, supporting educational and training programs for people of color, and defending their civil and social rights. The organization may work in collaboration with communities, government institutions, and other organizations to create a more equitable and just environment for all people, regardless of their skin color.



NGO Actions

Awareness Programs

We conduct talks, workshops, and awareness campaigns to educate the community about the importance of inclusion and the fight against racism.

Educational Support

We offer scholarships, tutoring, and educational resources to ensure that people of color have access to quality education and development opportunities.

Legal Defense

We provide legal advice and support to individuals who have been victims of racial discrimination, and we advocate for legislative changes to combat injustice.

Ways to Collaborate and Collaborations

Previous Alliances and Collaborations

In recent years, PCI has had the privilege of collaborating with various organizations and entities committed to promoting racial equality and social inclusion. These strategic alliances have allowed us to expand our impact and reach more diverse communities in our quest for a more equitable and just world. One of our most significant collaborations has been with the Network of Organizations for Racial Justice (ROJR), a national coalition dedicated to addressing racial disparities and promoting equity in all spheres of society. Through this partnership, we have co-organized awareness campaigns, community events, and educational programs aimed at challenging stereotypes and combating racial discrimination in our communities.

Ways to Support the Project

There are various ways you can support PCI in our mission to promote equality and inclusion. You can become a volunteer and participate in our activities and events, from awareness workshops to educational support programs. You can also make monetary or in-kind donations to help fund our initiatives and projects. Additionally, we are open to establishing partnerships with businesses, educational institutions, and other organizations that share our values and goals. Every contribution, no matter how small, makes a significant difference in the lives of the people we serve.
