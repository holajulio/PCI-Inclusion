Legal Defense

At PCI: Project for Commitment and Inclusion, we recognize that the fight against racial discrimination goes beyond awareness and education. Therefore, we have established a robust legal defense program dedicated to providing legal advice and comprehensive support to individuals who have been victims of racial discrimination. Our goal is not only to support individuals in their specific cases but also to drive legislative and structural changes that promote long-term racial equity and justice. We firmly believe in empowering affected communities through access to proper legal resources and strive to ensure that each person receives the necessary support to defend their civil and social rights.

Our services include:

  • Free legal advice for victims of racial discrimination.
  • Legal representation in civil rights cases.
  • Support in filing complaints with governmental bodies.
  • Assistance in mediation and conflict resolution.


Success Stories:

  • Case of Juan Pérez: We achieved significant compensation for Juan Pérez, who was discriminated against in his workplace due to his race. Our team of lawyers filed a lawsuit that resulted in a favorable settlement.
  • Legislative Reform: In collaboration with other organizations, we advocated for the implementation of new laws protecting people of color against discrimination in the educational and employment sectors.
