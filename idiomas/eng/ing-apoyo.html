Welcome to the Educational Support Website

The goal of educational support is to provide students with resources, strategies, and personalized assistance to improve their academic performance, personal development, and socio-emotional skills. This type of support is designed to complement formal education, helping students overcome obstacles, reach their full potential, and promote inclusive and equitable education.


Locations of Educational Support Programs

Schools and Educational Centers

Many educational institutions offer tutoring programs, remedial classes, and extracurricular activities to support students' learning.



The Joan XXIII School is an educational institution committed to academic excellence and the holistic development of its students. With a focus on forming values and skills, the school offers an inclusive and welcoming environment for students of various backgrounds and abilities. Its goals align with the mission of forming responsible, critical, and committed individuals in society.

Libraries

Many libraries offer tutoring programs and classes to help students of all ages. They are vital centers of educational resources and academic support.



The Bellvitge Library is a cultural and educational center located in the Bellvitge neighborhood of L'Hospitalet de Llobregat, a city near Barcelona, Spain. This library is part of the public library network of L'Hospitalet and offers a variety of services and resources to the local community.

Government Agencies
